About Jean Angel Gisemba

Jean Angel Gisemba is a remarkable 16-year-old Form Three student at Kenya High School – one of Kenya's most prestigious national schools. Hailing from Eldoret, Kenya in the heart of the Rift Valley, Jean embodies the spirit of resilience, excellence, and determination.

Her journey from Eldoret to the academic rigor of Kenya High School showcases her adaptability, intellectual curiosity, and unwavering commitment to personal growth.

Among her peers, Jean is celebrated for her infectious positivity, natural leadership abilities, and genuine passion for uplifting those around her. Whether organizing community clean-ups, leading study groups, or mentoring younger students, Jean consistently demonstrates the qualities of a true change-maker.

"From Eldoret to the classrooms of Kenya High, my journey is fueled by faith, hard work, and an unshakeable belief that education is the key to transforming lives." — Jean Angel Gisemba
